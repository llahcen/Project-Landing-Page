Mecca and the Ka'ba

One of the five pillars of Islam central to Muslim belief, Hajj is the pilgrimage to Mecca that every Muslim must make at least once in their lifetime if they are able

Some information about Hajj.

Hajj takes place during a specific week in the Islamic calendar, Dhu al-Hijjah.

While performing Hajj, pilgrims wear special garments called Ihram.

Hajj is a deeply spiritual experience for Muslims.

Completing Hajj is a life-changing experience for many Muslims..

"Whoever performs Hajj for Allah's pleasure and does not have sexual relations with his wife, and does not do evil or sins, then he will return (after Hajj free from all sins) as if he were born anew." (Sahih al-Bukhari)
